Cliveden

The Cliveden estate was constructed in 1767 and was owned by Benjamin Chew. It remained within the Chew family for seven generations before being sold.

The site is historically significant as one of the locations of the Battle of Germantown during the American Revolutionary War.

Guests can explore the Chew family papers and view Revolutionary War maps highlighting key events.

Check out Finding Black Families: Stories from the Chew Papers to learn more.
