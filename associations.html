Associations

American Library Association (ALA)

I am a student member of the American Library Association (ALA), the largest librarian organization in the world, dating back to the 1800s. ALA advocates for librarians and libraries, and promotes the rights to education, literacy, and intellectual freedom.

Pennsylvania Black Librarians Caucus (PaBLC)

I am a student member of the Pennsylvania Black Librarians Caucus (PaBLC), established in 2020. PaBLC's mission is to grow and strengthen the profession of librarianship by promoting education, career development, and recruitment efforts within Black communities.

Visit PaBLC

Black Caucus of the American Library Association (BCALA)

I am a new member of the Black Caucus of the American Library Association (BCALA). For over 50 years, BCALA has been advocating for the development, promotion, and improvement of library services and resources for African American communities across the United States, while also supporting the professional development of African American librarians.
