Welcome to Germantown Archiving Community

Archiving Community

The place where archiving and community come together. We are dedicated to preserving the rich history of Germantown, Philadelphia. We provide a platform for community members to discover stories, artifacts, and experiences. We believe that everyone has a story to tell, and we are here to help you discover stories untold or forgotten.

This website benefits historical enthusiast, historians, archivist and people who are interested in the local history of Germantown as well as those who are seekers of family history and lineage.
